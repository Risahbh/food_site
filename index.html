FOOD IN FARRUKHABAD

Food in Farrukhabad follows a typical Uttar Pradesh cuisine. Both veg and non-veg food items are enjoyed by the people. The non-veg recipes in the city mostly follow Mughal cooking methodology as Mughal recipes of biryani, kebabs, gravies and mutton recipes are much popular in the eateries of Farrukhabad.

Namkeen in Farrukhabad

Farrukhabad is one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of Namkeen snacks throughout the state and certain parts of the nation. The tourists in the city should never miss an opportunity to munch namkeen as it reflects a typical North Indian savoury snack. Namkeen comes out in many varieties like Namkeen dal, Mathri namkeen, Garma garam namkeen, ginger namkeen puri, namkeen barfi, namkeen adrak, namkeen idlai, namkeen rice balls, namkeen jalebi and many others which are all readily available in most of the sweet stalls and snacks stores in the city.

Sweet Supari in Farrukhabad

Sweet supari made out of beetle nut is another popular paan variety in Farrukhabad as the city is one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of sweet supari. Sweet supari is mostly enjoyed after a meal. Sweet supari acts as a mouth freshener after a heavy meal. Sweet supari wrapped in a beetle leaf is available in all paan shops in the city. Beverages in Farrukhabad

Beverages like coffee, tea and fruit drinks are quite popular in the city. Sharbats, chaas, lassi, rayta and thandai are very popular beverages in the city. The famous sharbats in the city are the lemon, rose, gurahi and khas ka sharbat. Here is the detailed list of beverages stores in the city.

Ice Cream Parlour in Farrukhabad

Ice cream is loved by people of all age group. The Vedilal ice cream shop in the city sells quality ice creams for retail and wholesale.

Bakery in Farrukhabad

The Shree Krishna bakery and confectionery store in the city sell bakery products and sweets with high quality apart from making yummy cakes for weddings and birthdays.

Papdi of Farrukhabad

Shivam chaat papdi in city is the most famous and delicious street food of farrukhabad which is healthy and unique and its been selling very last long.
